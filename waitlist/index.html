FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nexora OS? +
Nexora OS is the all-in-one operating system for content creators. It lets you schedule content across 5 platforms, repurpose videos with AI, manage brand deals with a CRM, and track analytics and earnings — all in one place instead of paying for 10 different tools.
Is Nexora OS free to use? +
Yes! Nexora OS is completely free during its beta period. No credit card required. Simply join the waitlist with your email and you'll get early access when we launch.
What platforms does Nexora OS support? +
Nexora OS currently supports YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X, TikTok, and LinkedIn. All five platforms are managed from one unified dashboard with a single calendar view for scheduling.
How does AI content repurposing work? +
Simply paste a YouTube URL or upload a video. Nexora OS's AI (powered by GPT-4) automatically analyzes your content and generates platform-specific posts for Twitter/X threads, Instagram captions, LinkedIn articles, and TikTok scripts in seconds.
What is the Brand Deals CRM feature? +
The Brand Deals CRM is a Kanban-style pipeline that helps you track every sponsorship deal from initial outreach to final payment. You can manage deal values, deadlines, auto-generate invoices, and track your total pipeline revenue — all without spreadsheets.
