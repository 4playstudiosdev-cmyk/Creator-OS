Description of Services

Nexora OS is an all-in-one creator operating system that provides the following core features to content creators:

  • Content Scheduler — Plan, write, and schedule posts across YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and TikTok from a single dashboard.
  • AI Repurposer — Use AI (powered by Groq LLaMA) to transform long-form content or YouTube videos into platform-optimized posts for multiple channels simultaneously.
  • Auto Clip Generator — Automatically identify viral moments in long videos and export 9:16 vertical clips for Shorts, Reels, and TikTok using Whisper AI transcription.
  • Brand Deals CRM — Manage your entire brand partnership pipeline from lead to paid, generate invoices, and track ROI with shareable tracking links.
  • Media Kit Builder — Create and share a professional creator media kit with a public URL at /u/[username].
  • Unified Inbox — View and manage DMs and comments from all connected social platforms in one inbox with AI-assisted reply suggestions.
  • Analytics Dashboard — Track cross-platform performance metrics, follower growth, engagement rates, and sponsorship ROI in one view.
  • Script Studio — Generate AI-written scripts for YouTube videos, podcasts, and ads.
  • AI Power Tools — Six standalone tools including an Idea Matrix, Hashtag Optimizer, Hook Generator, Title A/B Tester, Content Calendar, and Bio Writer.
  • Earnings Dashboard — Track revenue by source including AdSense, brand deals, affiliates, and memberships.

We reserve the right to modify, add, or remove features from the Services at any time. We will make reasonable efforts to communicate significant changes in advance.
